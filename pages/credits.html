    WELCOME TO THE GLO

    PROJECT OVERVIEW

    This academic project presents an immersive showcase website dedicated to Chief Keef. It highlights his career, cultural impact, and musical legacy through a modern web experience. The site includes interactive elements, such as a quiz and a memory game, offering a playful exploration of the artist's world.
